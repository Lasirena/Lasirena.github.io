Torrheim

About Me

  • Hey there! I'm Alice. Thanks for taking the time to check out my portfolio! :)

    You may have guessed this from my work already, but I am a multidisciplinary enthusiast who loves programming, art, and games. The creative process of bringing ideas to life is my favourite pastime - whether that means developing a new game project, painting an illustration of my next D&D character, or anything in between!

    For me, the most rewarding part of creativity is growth. I'm always seeking new challenges and ways to push myself to improve, and it's what drives a lot of my work.

    Aside from that, I love playing games - I could never list all my favourites, but recent ones include Elden Ring, Diablo 4, and Baldur's Gate 3 (I loved it way before I got to work on it!). I also play D&D and enjoy tabletop in general.

    When I'm not gaming, I'm probably either playing music (I play guitar and sing) or exploring new places. In particular, I enjoy seeing castles and cathedrals from the Gothic period when I get the chance, as well as historical museums (especially if they have medieval armour or weapon exhibits!).

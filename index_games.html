ABOUT ME

Developer, Artist, and a big fan of engaging game narratives.

With a year of games industry experience under my belt as a Technical Evangelist for Unity, I am looking for opportunities to grow and develop as a games artist.

I am currently in my graduate year of a Computer Science and Games Development course at City, University of London.

I have both a strong background in programming (C#, C++) as well as an unending passion for art which I have focused on developing in my personal time throughout the years.

Additionally, my time as an Evangelist provided me with a lot of valuable skills when it comes to content production, product presentation, and technical marketing.

Torrheim - a 2D narrative game

Global Game Jam (2019)

I worked on this Unity project with my colleague at the time during Global Game Jam. During the jam, I wanted to focus on producing art assets. I made all the 2D assets and animations, including characters, environments, props, and UI. They can be seen on my Artstation page.

3D Fighter

Hacksussex (2017)

This was a small fighter-type game I ended up making by myself in Unity over the course of Hacksussex 2017. A very simple 2-player game which let you fight each other with 2 controllers. Assets were sourced from the Asset Store.

Invoker - a VR game

Global Game Jam (2017)

Developed in Unity with a team of four, this was my very first game jam project. We put together a Virtual Reality game using a LeapMotion controller which scanned the movement and position of each finger, allowing the player to cast spells with specific gestures in order to fight off waves of enemy skeletons.

StarFall Kombat - Asteroids-inspired shooter

Personal Project (2016)

One of my very first game development projects, this 2D asteroids-like shooter was developed in Java using Processing. The game is named after the three other titles from which I have used a couple of assets, solely for educational purposes: Starcraft, Fallout, and Mortal Kombat.
